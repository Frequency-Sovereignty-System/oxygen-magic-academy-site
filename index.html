Education Still Needs a Human Center

教育仍然需要以人为中心

AI is changing how students find information, complete assignments, practice language, and prepare for future work. The challenge is no longer access to answers alone. The deeper challenge is whether learners can still understand, explain, improve, and responsibly use what they receive.

AI正在改变学生获取信息、完成作业、练习语言和准备未来工作的方式。 现在的问题已经不只是能否快速得到答案，而是学习者能否真正理解、 清楚表达、不断改进，并对所使用的内容负责。

AI can help learning move faster. Human beings must keep learning meaningful, responsible, and connected to real life. AI负责快，人负责对。

AI可以让学习更快，但人必须让学习保持真实、负责，并与现实生活连接。 AI负责快，人负责对。

What the Project Supports

项目支持什么

Oxygen Magic Academy focuses on educational needs that are becoming increasingly important as AI becomes part of everyday learning.

随着AI进入日常学习，氧气魔法学院关注正在变得越来越重要的教育需求。

01

Student Understanding

学生真实理解

Helping learners move beyond completed answers toward clearer understanding, personal expression, and confident use of knowledge.

帮助学习者从“完成答案”走向真正理解、个人表达与有信心地使用知识。

02

Teacher-Guided Learning

教师引导学习

Supporting the continuing value of teachers in observation, explanation, educational guidance, and student development.

强调教师在观察、解释、教育引导和学生成长中的持续价值。

03

Responsible AI Use

负责任地使用AI

Helping learners and institutions use AI as educational support while keeping academic responsibility and human oversight visible.

帮助学习者与机构把AI作为教育支持，同时保持学业责任和人类监督清晰可见。

04

Academic Development

学业能力发展

Supporting reading, writing, language learning, study confidence, academic participation, and continued learning.

支持阅读、写作、语言学习、学习信心、学业参与和持续学习能力。

05

Future Readiness

未来能力准备

Connecting education with communication, adaptability, technology awareness, career preparation, and participation in future work.

把教育与沟通能力、适应能力、技术理解、职业准备和未来工作参与连接起来。

06

Institutional Adaptation

教育机构适应

Supporting schools and educational programs as they consider how AI may be introduced without weakening their human and educational responsibilities.

支持学校与教育项目在引入AI时，仍然守住自身的人类责任与教育责任。

Designed for Real Learning Environments

面向真实学习环境

本项目可服务于不同学习环境，但不会用单一固定模式要求所有机构。 每个机构仍然对学生、教师、政策、技术、隐私和教育决策承担责任。

For Schools and Educational Organizations

面向学校与教育机构

Oxygen Magic Academy is available for early conversations with schools and educational organizations interested in AI-assisted learning, student understanding, language development, academic participation, and future readiness.

氧气魔法学院欢迎与关注AI辅助学习、学生理解、语言发展、 学业参与和未来能力准备的学校及教育机构进行早期交流。

Initial collaboration may begin with a limited pilot, educator discussion, institutional review, or a clearly defined learning group.

初步合作可以从小规模试点、教师交流、机构评估或明确的学习小组开始。

Start Small. Learn Clearly. 从小规模开始，清楚地验证。 A school does not need to replace its existing curriculum to begin exploring responsible AI-assisted learning. 学校不需要替换现有课程，也可以开始探索负责任的AI辅助学习。
