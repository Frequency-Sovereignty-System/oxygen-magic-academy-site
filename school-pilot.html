A School Pilot for AI-Assisted Learning

AI辅助学习学校试点

A small-scale educational pilot designed to support student understanding, teacher-guided learning, responsible AI use, academic development, and preparation for future learning and work.

这是一个小规模教育试点，旨在支持学生真实理解、教师引导学习、 负责任地使用AI、学业能力发展，以及未来学习和工作准备。

Who the Pilot Is For

试点适合哪些学校和学生

The pilot may be suitable for language schools, tutoring programs, adult learning programs, community education, after-school programs, and institutions exploring responsible AI-assisted learning.

本试点可适用于语言学校、辅导项目、成人学习项目、社区教育、 课后教育，以及正在探索负责任AI辅助学习的教育机构。

Language Learners

语言学习者

Students who need additional support with comprehension, expression, academic participation, and learning confidence.

需要加强理解、表达、学业参与和学习信心的学生。

Students Already Using AI

已经使用AI的学生

Students who already use AI but need clearer educational guidance, responsible use expectations, and teacher support.

已经使用AI，但需要更清楚的教育引导、负责任使用要求和教师支持的学生。

Future-Ready Learners

面向未来的学习者

Learners preparing for continued study, changing workplaces, and technology-supported learning environments.

正在为继续学习、变化中的工作环境和技术支持型学习环境做准备的学习者。

What the Pilot Supports

试点支持哪些能力

The pilot focuses on educational capacities that remain important as AI becomes part of everyday learning.

随着AI进入日常学习，本试点关注仍然重要的基本教育能力。

Student Understanding

学生真实理解

Supporting students in understanding and explaining the information they use.

支持学生理解并能够解释自己所使用的信息。

Teacher-Guided Learning

教师引导学习

Keeping teacher guidance, feedback, and educational oversight central.

保持教师引导、反馈和教育监督的核心作用。

Responsible AI Use

负责任地使用AI

Helping students use AI as educational support without replacing their own learning responsibility.

帮助学生把AI作为教育支持，而不是替代自身的学习责任。

Academic Development

学业能力发展

Supporting language learning, reading, writing, communication, and academic participation.

支持语言学习、阅读、写作、沟通和学业参与。

Correction and Improvement

纠正与改进

Helping students respond to feedback and improve the quality of their learning and work.

帮助学生回应反馈，并改进学习和任务成果的质量。

Future Readiness

未来能力准备

Connecting learning with adaptability, communication, responsible technology use, and future participation.

把学习与适应能力、沟通能力、负责任使用技术和未来参与连接起来。

Suggested Pilot Format

建议试点形式

  • 6–12 participating students
  • A four-week pilot period
  • One session per week
  • Approximately 45–60 minutes per session
  • One participating teacher or educator
  • No replacement of the school’s existing curriculum
  • 6–12名参与学生
  • 持续四周
  • 每周一次
  • 每次约45–60分钟
  • 一位参与教师或教育工作者
  • 不替代学校现有课程
Start Small and Observe Clearly 从小规模开始，清楚地观察 The purpose of the pilot is to help the school observe whether the program is useful for its students and educational environment before considering wider adoption. 试点的目的是帮助学校先观察本项目是否适合其学生和教育环境， 再决定是否考虑扩大采用。

What the School May Observe

学校可以观察什么

The pilot does not promise test-score improvement or guaranteed outcomes. It helps the school observe student participation, understanding, and responsible learning behavior.

试点不承诺考试成绩提升或固定结果， 而是帮助学校观察学生参与、理解和负责任学习行为的变化。

Clearer Explanation

表达更加清楚

Whether students can explain what they have learned and used.

学生是否能够解释自己学到了什么、使用了什么。

Greater Participation

参与度提高

Whether students participate more actively in learning activities.

学生是否更加主动地参与学习活动。

More Responsible Technology Use

更加负责任地使用技术

Whether students use AI with clearer awareness, responsibility, and teacher guidance.

学生是否在更清楚的意识、责任和教师引导下使用AI。
